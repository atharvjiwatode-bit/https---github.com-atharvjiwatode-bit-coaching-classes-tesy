IIT-JEE Mathematics Quick Revision Sheets

Quadratic Equation Roots

x = [-b ± √(b² - 4ac)] / 2a

Discriminant D = b² - 4ac. Real & distinct if D > 0, equal if D = 0, imaginary if D < 0.

Euler's Formula (Complex Numbers)

e^(iθ) = cosθ + i sinθ

Allows translation between polar coordinates and Cartesian form for complex ranks.

Limit Expansion (log)

ln(1 + x) = x - x²/2 + x³/3 - x&sup4;/4 + ...

Valid for -1 < x ≤ 1. Crucial for calculus evaluation limits.

NEET Chemistry Key Formulas

Ideal Gas Equation

P • V = n • R • T

R = 0.0821 L atm K⁻¹ mol⁻¹ or 8.314 J K⁻¹ mol⁻¹. Relates gas variables.

pH Calculation (Buffers)

pH = pKa + log([Salt]/[Acid])

Henderson-Hasselbalch equation. Crucial for equilibrium calculations.

Arrhenius Equation (Rate Constant)

k = A • e^(-Ea / RT)

Ea is activation energy. R is gas constant. T is absolute temperature.
